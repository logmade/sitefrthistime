Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of ogn Virtualizer?
The ogn Virtualizer eliminates any unauthorized access to your hardware data by third-party software, operating at the kernel level. Please note that the use of ognVM for any illegal or fraudulent activities is strictly prohibited.
Is it detectable?
Our advanced technology ensures that ogn Virtualizer remains undetectable by current anti-cheat systems and tracking methods.
Do I need to reinstall Windows?
In most cases, no. Xanax Virtualizer works without requiring a full Windows reinstallation, making it quick and convenient to use. For valorant and extreme Fortnite bans, a reinstall may be needed.
How long does the process take?
The virtualization process typically takes 3-7 minutes for most users, depending on your system configuration.
Will I receive a refund if the virtualizer is incompatible with my PC?
Absolutely. We offer refunds for cases where the virtualizer does not function on your computer.
What can I expect after making a purchase?
Upon completing your purchase, you will receive a download link along with a unique license key via email.
What should I do if I lose my license key?
You are responsible for the security of your license key. Unfortunately, we cannot provide reimbursement or issue a replacement key.
Are all motherboards compatible with the virtualizer?
Yes, Xanax Virtualizer supports all motherboards.
Is there a risk of my PC becoming inoperable during virtualization?
No, Xanax Virtualizer is designed with safety in mind and includes failsafes to prevent any potential damage to your PC.
How many times can I use the virtualizer?
You can utilize the virtualizer an unlimited number of times, provided that your license key remains valid.
Is the virtualization process user-friendly?
Yes, the process is straightforward: simply enable virtualization and disable secure boot, then launch the virtualizer and log in.
